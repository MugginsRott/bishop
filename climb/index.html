Bishop Bouldering

The day I sent the most esthetic Boulder I have climbed yet, started like any other day in bishop. Stevie and I got a normal 11:00am start at the Grandpa Peabody boulder. We began with a little (the tallest boulder around, standing at 40’ high) warm up V0 “Essential Peabody”, to get things moving and continued with its neighboring V2 “NorthFace Direct”. After a while with no plan other then to stink around the front side to wait for Stefan and Domini, Stevie decided to give the far right problem “Go Granny Go” V5 a few goes. She sussed the beta and gave’er hell all while enduring the standard buttermilks hurricane force winds blowing pads away and generally bringing havoc to the scene. To our left a guy decided to send his first V10 whilst three of us ran after his pads blowing away in the wind. After some time, Stevie made the call to move on ( she sent the following session with ease) .....
